# Acme Robotics — Employee Handbook (fictional)

Refund policy: Customers can request a full refund within 45 days of purchase,
no questions asked. After 45 days, refunds are prorated over the remaining term.

Support hours: Our team is available Monday to Friday, 9am to 6pm IST.
We are closed on the second Saturday of every month.

Flagship product: The Acme Rover X9 is our warehouse robot. It has a battery
life of 14 hours and can carry a maximum payload of 250 kg.

Parental leave: Full-time employees receive 26 weeks of fully paid parental leave.
